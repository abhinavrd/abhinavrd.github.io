Course Plan
Week Topics Evaluation
1. Vectors I: position, force and moment, the dot product of two vectors, vector cross product
2. Vectors II: Index notation, problems
3. Vectors III: Moment, problems Quiz 1
4. Dynamics in 1D and Free-Body Diagrams: Force and motion, energy methods, harmonic oscillator, coupled motions, collisions, Equivalent force sets, Center of mass and gravity, Interactions, forces & partial FBDs, Contact forces: Sliding, rolling, and collisions, Friction: perspectives on friction laws
5. Particles in space: Dynamics of a particle in space, momentum and energy, central-force motion and celestial mechanics
6. Straight line motion: 1D motion and pulleys, 1D motion w/ 2D & 3D forces
7. Circular motion of a particle: Circular motion kinematics, dynamics of particle in circular motion Quiz 2
8. Many particles in space: Coupled particle motion, Particle collisions
9. Midsem
10. Circular motion of a rigid object: 2D rigid-object rotation, 2D rigid-object angular velocity, polar moment of inertia, dynamics of rigid-object planar circular motion
11. Planar motion of an object I: Rigid object kinematics, mechanics of a rigid-object
12. Planar motion of an object II: Kinematics of rolling and sliding, mechanics of contact, collisions
13. Time-varying basis vectors I: Polar coordinates and path coordinates, rotating frames and their base vectors, general formulas for velocity and acceleration Quiz 3
14. Time-varying basis vectors II: Kinematics of 2-D mechanisms, advanced kinematics of planar motion
15. Constrained particles and rigid objects: Mechanics of a constrained particle, one-degree-of-freedom 2-D mechanisms, multi-dof 2D mechanisms Quiz 4
16. Fixed-axis rotation in 3D: 3D description of circular motion, dynamics of fixed-axis rotation, moment of inertia matrices
17. Fixed-axis rotation in 3D II: Using the inertia matrix, dynamic balance
18. 3D rigid-object dynamics: Velocity and acceleration of points on a 3D rigid object, 3D dynamics of a rigid body, Theorems for Systems Quiz 5
19. Review I
20. Review II
