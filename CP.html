Course Plan
Week Topics Evaluation
1 Vectors: position, force and moment:
Notation and addition, the dot product of two vectors, vector cross product, moment, solving vector equations
2 Free-Body Diagrams:
Equivalent force sets, center of mass and gravity, interactions, forces & partial FBDs, contact forces: sliding, rolling, and collisions
3 Statics of one object:
Static equilibrium of a particle, equilibrium of one object, equilibrium with frictional contact, internal forces, 3D statics 		Quiz 1 (CO1)
4 Trusses and frames:
Method of joints, the method of sections, solving trusses on a computer, frames and structures, advanced truss concepts: determinacy
5 Transmissions and mechanisms:
Springs, force amplification, mechanisms
6 Dynamics in 1D:
Force and motion in 1D, energy methods in 1D, a mass and spring: the harmonic oscillator, coupled motions in 1D, collisions in 1D 		Quiz 2 (CO2)
7 Particles in space:
Dynamics of a particle in space, momentum and energy, central-force motion and celestial mechanics
8 Many particles in space:
Coupled particle motion, particle collisions

Straight line motion:
1D motion and pulleys, 1D motion w/ 2D & 3D forces
9 Circular motion of a particle:
Circular motion kinematics, dynamics of particle circular motion
10 Circular motion of a rigid object:
2D rigid-object rotation, 2D rigid-object angular velocity, polar moment of inertia, dynamics of rigid-object planar circular motion 		Quiz 3 (CO3)
11 Planar motion of an object:
Rigid object kinematics, mechanics of a rigid-object, kinematics of rolling and sliding, mechanics of contact, collisions
12 Time-varying basis vectors:
Polar coordinates and path coordinates, rotating frames and their base vectors, general formulas for v and a, kinematics of 2-D mechanisms, advanced kinematics of planar motion
13 [Lab] Computation: Algebra, ODEs, etc.
ODEs: Ordinary Differential Equations, Numerical solution of ODEs, Computation and graphics 		Quiz 4 (CO4) and Assignment (CO3, CO4)
