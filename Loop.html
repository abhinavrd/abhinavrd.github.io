String shooter

How can a moving loop of string, such as a string shooter, keep its shape while gravity pulls it down and air pushes against it? If the string has only tensile stiffness, the loop cannot pass vertical without singularity. We try to uncover what allows the loop to stay steady without breaking.

  • 2nd International Conference on Highly Flexible Slender Structures 2025, Kaiserslautern, Germany. (Talk by James Hanna)
